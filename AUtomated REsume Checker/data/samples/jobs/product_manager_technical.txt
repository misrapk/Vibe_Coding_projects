Technical Product Manager
Company: TechBridge Innovations
Location: Los Angeles, CA
Type: Full-time

Job Description:
We are looking for a Technical Product Manager who can bridge the gap between business requirements and technical implementation. You will be working closely with our engineering team to define product roadmaps and deliver high-value features.

Requirements:
- 3-5 years of experience in Product Management or Technical Leadership.
- Strong technical background with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field.
- Experience with Agile methodologies and Scrum.
- Ability to make data-driven decisions and communicate technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
- Experience leading product development cycles from ideation to launch.

Preferred Skills:
- Experience with SQL and data analysis tools.
- Previous experience as a software engineer.
- Strong user-centric design sense.
